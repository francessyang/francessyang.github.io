ARCH STROLL THRU TOKYO
ARCH STROLL THRU TOKYO
  • Project by Frances Yang
  • Date Travlled: Summer 2019

Hi! My name is Frances. I'm an Junior studying Architecture at UC Berkeley. I travelled to Japan this past summer with my sister. We wanted to do something different besides visiting the Tokyo Tower and draining my wallet on things we probably don't need, so I did a little research and planned out a route to visit some of the interesting architecture that Tokyo has to offer.

This is a little interactive map for you to come along with me as I reminisce my time strolling through the streets of Tokyo, admiring all the beautiful architecture.
