ARCH STROLL THRU TOKYO
TOD'S
  • Architect: Toyo Ito & Associates
  • Year: 2002

After another 5min walk, you will see two very distinct
buildings across the street. The glass building with white
crisscrossing patterns is by the famous architect, Toyo Ito.

The white concrete braces micmics the criscrossing branches
of the trees lining the streets of Omotesando. The light
shines through the gaps in between, creating a really
interesting visual expereince from the inside.

I encrouage you to step into the buildings! Even though
I wasn't going to buy anything, I still went in becuase
I did not want to pass off the opportunity to look at the
building from the inside. I didn't go to the very top floor,
but I heard there's a terrace roof garden there.
